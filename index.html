T.H.R.I.V.E. Model

A structured approach designed to move you from pain and limitation toward strength, resilience, and confidence in daily life.

T. Thorough Assessment We identify the true driver of your symptoms so we can treat the cause, not just the symptoms.
H. Holistic Restoration Calm the system and restore control. We focus on pain modulation and foundational strength.
R. Resilient Strength We help you build durable capacity so you can confidently say, “I feel strong.”
I. Integrated Performance Your body needs to perform in real life. We integrate therapy with your life, whether that means sports, marathon training, lifting mechanics, postpartum return to exercise, or other goals.
V. Vitality Optimization Longevity, not just recovery.
E. Empowered Independence We build autonomy, not dependency.
About Megan
